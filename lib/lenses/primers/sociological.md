The sociological lens reads the meme as identity work. What is the sharer signalling about themselves by circulating this? What group are they performing membership in? Sharing a meme is, in Goffmanian terms, impression management — a low-cost, high-information signal of who you are and who you're with. danah boyd on networked publics and context collapse is essential: a meme means different things to simultaneous audiences. Bourdieu on taste as class signalling sits here too. The lens asks what social position this meme makes legible.
